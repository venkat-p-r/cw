Awards:

    The superior quality of the training offered at CPAT is evidenced clearly by the fact that its students have topped the Regional Skill Competitions, besides winning National level awards. Further, CPAT students have received excellent commendations from their Industrial Employers for their high motivation and initiative, their innovative ideas and their expertise in carrying them out.

IN-PLANT TRAINING PROGRAM:

    During the 4th and 7th semesters, students go through in-plant training in state of the art industries. During the 7th semester students also complete industrial projects in their field. These experiences give them a direct understanding of industrial manufacturing systems and processes, and quality concepts. This enhances their employability.
